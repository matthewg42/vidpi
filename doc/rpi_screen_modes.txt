In config.txt there are two values: hdmigroup and htmimode.

Here are some common combinations and what they mean:

group	mode	screen config
1	1	VGA (640x480)
1	4	720p
1	5	1080i
1	6	480i
1	16	1080p
1	5	1080i
2	8	800x600 60 Hz
2	16	1024x768 60 Hz
2	18	1024x768 75 Hz
